About

Welcome! I am a first-year DPhil (PhD) student in Politics at Nuffield College, University of Oxford. My research explores political behaviour in advanced industrial democracies, with a particular focus on the formation of attitudes and preferences. I usually work with panel data using causal inference methods.

Before starting my PhD, I obtained an MPhil from Lincoln College, University of Oxford, and a BSc from University College London.
